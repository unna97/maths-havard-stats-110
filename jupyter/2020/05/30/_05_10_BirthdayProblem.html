Let the probablity that two people in the group with n_party share a birthday be p.

Then, probablity that they won't share a birthday is $q = 1 - p$

Selecting r objects out of n objects = $C(n,r) = (n)!/((n - r)!*(r)!)$

:space_invader:

Now,

number of people in party, $r = 2$,

days in year, $n = 356$

then:

$C(356, 2) =365!/((365 - 2)! * (2)!)$

def factorial(n):
  ans = 1
  for i in range(1, n + 1):
    ans *= i
  return ans
def p_birthday(n, r):
  q = (factorial(n) /(factorial(n - r) * factorial(r)))
  print(factorial(r))
  print(q)
  return (1 - q)
